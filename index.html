Profile

Modelling and analytics for real public health decisions

Mulugeta Geremew Geleso is a mathematical and statistical modeller with more than a decade of experience applying advanced analytics, epidemiological modelling, geospatial methods, machine learning, forecasting, and visualization to strengthen public health decision-making.

He currently serves as Head of the Data Analytics, Modelling and Visualization Division at the National Data Management and Analytics Center for Health, Ethiopian Public Health Institute. His work supports national priorities including malaria, measles, TB, immunization, outbreak preparedness, health systems analytics, and policy-oriented evidence generation.
