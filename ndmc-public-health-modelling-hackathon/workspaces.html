How to send your work

  1. Open your branchUse the workspace assigned to your family.
  2. Add your filesInclude code, README, slides, outputs and approved data materials.
  3. Commit clearlyUse a short message that explains what changed.
  4. Push or uploadPush if authorised, or use a fork and pull request.
  5. Submit for reviewOpen a pull request to hackathon-workspaces.
