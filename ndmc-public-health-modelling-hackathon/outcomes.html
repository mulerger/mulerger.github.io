Hackathon Outcomes

The six-day programme produced four initial analytical and modelling prototypes shaped around programme questions and decision needs.

Work developed

  • Zero-dose immunization — woreda-level estimation and mapping for programme prioritization.
  • Antimalarial medicine forecasting and allocation — demand forecasting and allocation analysis for programme planning.
  • Measles alert rules — threshold analysis for earlier, interpretable surveillance alerts.
  • Measles transmission strategies — scenario analysis for transmission control and elimination.

GitHub workspaces

Each family has a dedicated branch for its code, documented data, methods, outputs, prototype, report and final presentation.

Open the workspace guide.

Follow-up

The families will refine the analyses, validate the methods with programme specialists and prepare the outputs for practical use and public release where appropriate.
