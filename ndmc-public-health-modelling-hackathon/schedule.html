Schedule

The programme moved from challenge framing and team formation to prototype development, presentation and follow-up over six focused days.

Day 1 · 1 July

Frame the challenges and form families

Opening, challenge presentations, clarification with programme teams, participant introductions, skills matching and family formation.

Day 2 · 2 July

Plan the work and set up collaboration

Git/GitHub refresher, dialogue with decision-makers, repository setup, data review, modelling questions and family work plans.

Day 3 · 3 July

Build the first prototype

Data preparation, exploratory analysis, model design, coding, visualization and the first working version.

Day 4 · 4 July

Review and refine

Technical mentoring, prototype refinement, programme feedback, interpretation, testing and documentation.

Day 5 · 5 July

Finalise outputs

Final analysis, demonstration flow, policy message, figures, slides, README files and submission readiness.

Day 6 · 6 July

Present and agree next steps

Family presentations, feedback, recognition, closing, GitHub submission and agreement on technical follow-up.
