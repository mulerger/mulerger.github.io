NDMC Public Health Policy Modelling Hackathon 2026

Bridging modelling and policy for better public-health decisions

1–6 July 2026Stay Easy Plus HotelAddis Ababa, Ethiopia

Hosted by Ethiopian Public Health Institute

A six-day collaborative event where public-health specialists, modellers, data scientists and developers worked together on four priority health challenges for Ethiopia.

Background

From health data to decisions

Public-health decisions increasingly depend on timely analysis that combines surveillance, programme, population and contextual data. Useful evidence emerges when decision-makers, domain specialists, modellers and developers shape the questions and solutions together.

NDMC convened the hackathon to connect those perspectives and develop practical, open and reproducible approaches around immunisation, malaria and measles priorities.

Each family brought together policy context, epidemiology, statistics, modelling and software development.
